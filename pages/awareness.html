Cow-Awareness

"Save The Cow,Start Now"

This video highlights the importance of proper cow care, healthy living conditions, and the role of cows in supporting agriculture and rural livelihoods.


Following these simple do’s and don’ts can improve cow health, increase productivity, and promote responsible animal care.

✅ Do's

  • Provide clean and fresh drinking water daily.
  • Maintain a clean and dry shelter for cows.
  • Give balanced and nutritious feed.
  • Ensure regular vaccination and medical checkups.
  • Handle cows gently and with care.
  • Isolate sick cows to prevent disease spread.
  • Keep feeding areas clean and hygienic.
    

❌Don'ts

  • Do not keep cows in dirty or overcrowded places.
  • Do not ignore signs of illness or weakness.
  • Do not overfeed or give spoiled food.
  • Do not leave cows unattended on roads.
  • Do not delay medical treatment.
  • Do not use harsh or violent behavior.
